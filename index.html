About

Bio

Kevin Haulihan prioritizes storytelling that surprises and moves viewers while entertaining them — moving deftly between linear broadcast and new media, long form and short.

With LA Times Studios, Kevin has developed and created Emmy-winning content for major brands like Apple, Amazon Music, Disney, and Pfizer — including work in the scripted-podcast space.

Prior to this, he was a Director of Development for World of Wonder Productions, where he developed series in both scripted and unscripted across multiple formats.

Other credits include RuPaul's Drag Race, Best Week Ever, No, You Shut Up!, Hoppus on Music, and The Trixie & Katya Show.
